Infopark Kochi, a perfect destination to house your IT/ITES Business in Kochi, features all world class infrastructures for an IT Park.

Started in 2004, the Park owned by the Government of Kerala, is the single window for setting up your IT/ITES Business. Formed with the objective of creating Infrastructure facilities for IT/ITES Companies in the Central Kerala Region, Kochi. Located towards the eastern side of Kochi, at Kakkanad, the Park sprawls over 260 acres of land in this main hub. Two satellite campuses- at Koratty in Thrissur district and Cherthala in Alappuzha district, are also operational within a reach of 45 minutes from the main hub. With over 9.2 million of Built-up area, the park created all the infrastructure settings needed for a balanced work place.
